What is Pay by Wallet and how is it different from EZ-Link Wallet?

The newest feature of the enhanced EZ-Link Wallet, Pay by Wallet is a digital prepaid Mastercard within the EZ-Link App.

Once activated, consumers will be able to use their EZ-Link Wallet for contactless payments in-store, online and overseas at over 80 million Mastercard merchants, in addition to the existing SGQR and Alipay+ acceptance points.

Find out more about EZ-Link Wallet here.

How do I enable my Pay by Wallet?

Step 1: Ensure that you have already signed up for an EZ-Link Wallet

Step 2. Click on the “Pay by Wallet” banner in your Wallet details page

Step 3:  Perform the necessary identity verification checks

Step 4: Once all verification checks are complete, your “Pay by Wallet” will be activated immediately.

Users will now have access to a virtual account that comes with a virtual card, located within the Pay by Wallet feature.

Are there any eligibility requirements to use Pay by Wallet?

Anyone with a valid NRIC or FIN is eligible to apply for Pay by Wallet.  Singaporeans are required to perform the necessary identity checks via Singpass while FIN card holders are required to submit their Proof of Residence (with documents dated less than 6 months ago), in addition to their Myinfo details.

There are no income requirements to enable Pay by Wallet.

Is my EZ-Link Wallet secure from phishing scams? How do I know if my personal data is safe?

The EZ-Link Wallet implements security practices and policies adopted by the payment card industry to ensure prevention of theft of credit/debit card information. Furthermore, your authorization is always required before any payment is confirmed.

All EZ-Link Pay by Wallet users have the option to block or unblock their digital card within the app. Users can also hide sensitive card details (e.g. account number, expiry date, and CVC code) on their EZ-Link Wallet, and take the additional precaution of using biometric authentication to access the EZ-Link App on their mobile device.
